There are three Landscape editions to choose from for managing your Ubuntu Pro enabled Ubuntu Instances. Landscape SaaS is a multi-tenant cloud service, and is where the newest features first arrive. Landscape is also available as a Managed Service for customers that want a turnkey SaaS solution as a single tenant deployment. Self-hosted Landscape can be deployed on any cloud, on-premises, and even in an airgapped environment. A new Landscape release arrives every 6 months and follows the Ubuntu release calendar, with interim releases supported for 6 months and LTS releases security maintained for 10 years.
